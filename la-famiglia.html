La Famiglia Menu

The Challenge

To create a menu for a fictional restaurant called La Famiglia.

The Solution

The concept behind the menu for La Famiglia centres around a family tree, emphasizing the family's history and story. Rosemary and thyme, key herbs in Italian cooking, highlight the fresh ingredients used throughout the menu. To keep a home-style dining experience, I've limited the menu items and embraced a family-style dining approach for sharing. The back of the menu takes a more literal approach to a family tree, highlighting each family member.
