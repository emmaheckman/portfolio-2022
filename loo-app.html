The Challenge

Finding a public washroom in Downtown Vancouver can be challenging. People with kids, non-binary people, trans people and people with disabilities struggle even more to find a bathroom that will fit their needs. How might we create an app that will help users locate a washroom according to their washroom preferences?

The Solution

LOO is an app that helps users locate, review, rate and choose bathroom preference in the Downtown area.
