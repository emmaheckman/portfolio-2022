Pure and Simple

The Challenge

To create eye-catching packaging that will appeal to milk drinkers and will persuade milk alternative drinkers to switch back to milk.

The Solution

My solution was to put only the necessary content on the bottle to not distract from the cleanness of the bottle. I chose a clear glass rounded bottle that would show off the purity of the milk and allow the white of the milk to shine through the cow in the logo.
