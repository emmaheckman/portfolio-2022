Make Friends with Vancouver Micro Site

The Challenge

To create a landing page for Destination Vancouver’s new campaign, Make Friends with Vancouver. The campaign reaches out to residents of Sydney, Australia, to encourage tourism to Vancouver. The microsite showcases the range of Vancouver activities that would appeal to Australians, and encourages people from around Sydney to sign a petition to make Vancouver one of Sydney’s official Friendship Cities.

The Solution

Working with Teresa to design a landing page that fits graphically with the campaign, taking into consideration Destination Vancouver's website grid, button styles and existing layout.

Agency: Will Creative
