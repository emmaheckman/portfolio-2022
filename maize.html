Maize Website

Project Team

Tiffany Zhong (Art Direction), Lucy Benson (Illustration), Myself (UX, UI)

The Challenge

To create a website for an innovative textile brand that turns leftover cornhusks into strong sustainable workwear that lasts.

The Solution

The goal of the website was to achieve a modern, bold, and gritty yet high-end aesthetic for a modern workwear company. We aimed to present the products in a visually appealing manner by incorporating gritty textures, bold photography, and illustrations that reflect the gritty essence of MAIZE’s brand. To emphasize MAIZE’s identity and values, we opted for an experimental approach on the About page while ensuring a clean design on the product and shop pages, allowing the clothing items to take center stage.
