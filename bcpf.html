BC Parks Foundation Website

The Challenge

Create a website for the rebrand of BC Parks Foundation.

The Solution

My role as a graphic and production designer was to build out pages and modules based on the rebrand and the core pages Taylor had designed. I created new pages, including the Article page, Donate page, and Contact page, plus various site modules, working with Taylor to maintain a cohesive look and feel across the project. I also supported the production by creating mobile versions of the desktop pages, handling content entry in Sanity, and working with our in-house developer.

Agency: Burnkit

Ryan Gravito (Creative Director), Taylor Peters (Designer)
