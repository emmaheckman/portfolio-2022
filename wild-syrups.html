Wild Syrups

Packaging

About Wild

Wild is a line of naturally derived high-end syrups that lets dedicated foodies experience distinctive West Coast flavors in their fizzy drinks or cocktails. Each berry or plant provides a unique taste experience from a different West Coast region.

The Challenge

To develop a syrup brand that creates unique flavors that evoke regions on the West Coast, so that users can feel a connection to their local environment through their beverage.

The Solution

A small local syrup brand that creates syrups for local high-end restaurants, bars and shops. The watercoulor on the packaging elicits the feeling of being in nature and the openess and senses you experience in BC.
