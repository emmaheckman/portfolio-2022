Cents App

UX / UI

The Challenge

To create an engaging fintech app for pre-teens.

The Solution

I wanted this app to be a place where pre-teens could have fun and learn about money while saving towards a goal. I chose colourful cheerful graphics as well as a clean and easy way to navigate through the app. The colourful line graphics give a sense of control but at the same time a sense of playfulness to experiment and try new things.
